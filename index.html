Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Johannes!

Computer Science Student & Etsy Seller from Freiburg, Germany

I started my Etsy shop just 5 months ago, and like many sellers, I quickly ran into a frustrating problem: there was no good way to track my inventory. Spreadsheets were a mess, manual tracking was error-prone, and existing solutions were either too expensive or didn't fit my needs.

So I did what any developer would do - I built my own solution. What started as a simple inventory tracker has evolved into a comprehensive management system with automated order syncing, profit analysis, production planning, and so much more.

I'm currently studying computer science at the University of Freiburg, and I'm constantly improving this software based on real-world use in my own shop. Every feature you see here solves a real problem I've encountered as an Etsy seller.
